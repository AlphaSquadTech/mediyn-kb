# Admin & Settings

Mediyn gives clinic administrators tools to manage practice information, oversee sessions, and maintain control over clinical operations.

## What You Can Do

- View and update your clinic's business information
- Manage sessions at an administrative level, including bulk operations
- Oversee clinical records and related data
- Maintain your clinic's profile details for invoices and superbills

## Key Concepts

**Clinic information** — Your practice's official business details. These include:
- Clinic name
- Address (street, city, state, zip code)
- Phone number
- Business email
- Tax identification number
- Clinic logo

These details appear on invoices, superbills, and other documents generated by Mediyn.

**Administrative session management** — Clinic administrators can perform session-level operations that go beyond what therapists can do on their own. This includes removing sessions and their related clinical records when necessary.

**Cascade removal** — When an administrator removes a session, all related records are also removed. This includes clinical artifacts, uploaded files, processing records, and worksheet materials. This ensures no orphaned data is left behind.
