# Tips for Admin & Settings

## Recommendations

- **Keep clinic information current** — Your clinic name, address, phone, email, and tax details appear on invoices and superbills. Update them promptly whenever anything changes.

- **Upload your clinic logo** — A professional logo on your documents builds trust with patients and insurance companies.

- **Use session removal sparingly** — Removing sessions is permanent and cannot be undone. Only use this feature when absolutely necessary, such as for compliance or correcting significant errors.

- **Always double-check before bulk removal** — Removing all sessions for a patient deletes every related record. Verify that this is the right course of action before confirming.

- **Standardize templates across your practice** — Promote approved templates so all therapists use the same formats. This improves consistency and makes clinical reviews easier.

- **Review templates before promoting** — Once a template is promoted, all therapists can use it. Make sure it meets your practice's standards first.

- **Audit administrative actions regularly** — Check the audit log to review session removals and other administrative actions. This supports accountability and compliance.

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I undo a session removal?**
A: No. Removing a session is permanent. All related clinical records, uploads, and worksheets are also removed and cannot be recovered.

**Q: What happens to invoices and superbills when I remove a session?**
A: Session removal does not automatically void related invoices or superbills. You should review and void them separately if needed.

**Q: Who can remove sessions?**
A: Only clinic administrators can remove sessions through the admin tools. Therapists do not have access to this feature.

**Q: Why do I have to confirm bulk session removal?**
A: The confirmation step is a safety measure to prevent accidental data loss. Removing all sessions for a patient is a significant action and cannot be reversed.

**Q: Can therapists change clinic information?**
A: No. Only clinic administrators can view and update clinic business information.

**Q: How do I update the tax ID on my invoices?**
A: Go to Settings, select Clinic Information, and update the tax identification number. Future invoices and superbills will use the updated value.
