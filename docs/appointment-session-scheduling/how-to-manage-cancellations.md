# How to Manage Cancellations

Handle session cancellations, late cancels, and no-shows with clear policies and automatic fee tracking.

## Steps

### Setting up a cancellation policy

1. Go to your practice settings in Mediyn.
2. Navigate to cancellation policies.
3. Create a new policy.

**You'll need to provide:**

- Policy name (for example, "Standard 24-Hour Policy")
- Required notice period (in hours before the session)
- Late cancellation fee amount
- No-show fee amount

4. Save the policy.

You can also update an existing policy by changing the name, notice period, fees, or status. Policies can be set to **Active** or **Archived**.

### Canceling a session

1. Open the session you need to cancel.
2. Select the cancel option.

**You can also:**

- Add a reason for the cancellation

3. Confirm the cancellation.

Mediyn automatically checks your cancellation policy. If the patient cancels after the required notice period, the session is flagged as a late cancel and a fee invoice may be created.

### Marking a no-show

1. Open the session where the patient did not attend.
2. Select the no-show option.
3. Confirm.

If your cancellation policy includes a no-show fee, Mediyn automatically creates a fee invoice.

## What to Expect

**When you cancel a session:**

- The session moves to the **Cancelled** status
- Mediyn records who canceled and when
- If it qualifies as a late cancel, the session is flagged accordingly
- A late-cancel fee invoice is created if your policy requires one

**When you mark a no-show:**

- The session moves to the **Cancelled** status
- The session is marked as a no-show
- A no-show fee invoice is created if your policy requires one

## Good to Know

- The cancellation policy compares the cancellation time against the required notice hours. Canceling inside the notice window triggers the late-cancel fee.
- You can have multiple cancellation policies (for example, different policies for different session types).
- Archiving a policy does not affect sessions already governed by it.
- Both therapists and clinic administrators can cancel sessions and mark no-shows.
- Fee invoices are created automatically. You do not need to manually charge the patient.
