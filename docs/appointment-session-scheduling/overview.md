# Appointment & Session Scheduling

Mediyn gives you everything you need to schedule sessions, manage your availability, and let patients book online.

## What You Can Do

- Create and configure session types for your practice
- Schedule individual, group, and telehealth sessions
- Set up recurring session schedules for regular patients
- Manage cancellations, reschedules, and no-shows
- Define cancellation policies with late-cancel and no-show fees
- Set your weekly availability templates
- View your daily schedule at a glance
- Enable online booking so patients can self-schedule
- Manage booking requests from new and existing patients
- Offer prepaid session packages

## Key Concepts

**Session**
A scheduled appointment between a therapist and a patient. Each session has a start time, end time, session type, and a status that tracks where it is in the workflow.

**Session Type**
A category of appointment your practice offers. Each session type has a name, duration, rate, and procedure code. Examples: "Individual Therapy - 50 min" or "Group Session - 90 min."

**Recurring Sessions**
A repeating schedule that automatically creates sessions for a patient. You define the frequency, day, time, and duration. Mediyn generates sessions up to 90 days in advance.

**Cancellation Policy**
Rules that define what happens when a session is canceled or missed. Policies set the required notice period and fees for late cancellations and no-shows.

**Online Booking**
A public booking page where patients can view your availability and request appointments. You control which session types are available, payment requirements, and whether bookings need your approval.

**Booking Request**
When a patient books online, their request may be auto-confirmed or held for your review, depending on your settings.

**Session Package**
A prepaid bundle of sessions that patients can purchase. Packages offer a set number of sessions at a defined price.

**Availability Template**
Your recurring weekly schedule that defines when you are open for appointments. Online booking uses these templates to show available time slots.
