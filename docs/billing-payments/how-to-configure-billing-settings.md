# How to Configure Billing Settings

Mediyn lets clinic administrators customize how billing and invoicing work for your practice.

![Billing Settings](../images/clinic/settings-billing.png)

## Steps

1. Go to **Settings** in Mediyn.
2. Select **Billing Settings**.
3. Review and update your preferences.
4. Save your changes.

## Available Settings

You can configure the following options:

- **Currency** — Choose the currency for all invoices and charges in your practice.

- **Auto-create invoices** — When turned on, Mediyn automatically creates an invoice when a session is approved. This saves you from manually creating invoices after each session.
  - Available choices: Yes or No

- **Auto-charge payments** — When turned on, Mediyn automatically charges the patient's saved payment method as soon as an invoice is created.
  - Available choices: Yes or No

- **Auto-create superbills** — When turned on, Mediyn automatically generates a superbill when a session is approved. This is helpful for practices that routinely provide superbills for insurance reimbursement.
  - Available choices: Yes or No

## What to Expect

After saving your settings, Mediyn will apply them to all future sessions and invoices. Existing invoices are not affected by changes to these settings.

## Good to Know

- Only clinic administrators can view and update billing settings.
- Turning on auto-charge requires that your patients have a saved payment method on file. If no payment method is saved, the invoice will be created but the charge will not go through automatically.
- You can combine auto-create invoices with auto-charge for a fully hands-free billing workflow.
- Changes take effect immediately for all new sessions going forward.
