# How to View Invoices

Mediyn keeps a complete record of all invoices so you can review charges, track payments, and download copies.

## Steps

1. Go to the **Billing** section in Mediyn.
2. Select **Invoices** to see your full invoice list.
3. Use the available options to narrow down your results:
   - **Patient** — Show invoices for a specific patient
   - **Status** — Show only invoices at a certain stage (see below)
4. Select any invoice to view its full details.

## Understanding Invoice Details

Each invoice includes:

- **Invoice number** — A unique sequential number (for example, INV-2026-0001)
- **Patient name and address**
- **Therapist name, NPI, and license number**
- **Clinic name, address, phone, and email**
- **Line items** — Individual charges on the invoice
- **Subtotal, tax, and total amount**
- **Currency**
- **Date created and date paid** (if applicable)

## Invoice Stages

Your invoices move through these stages:

- **Draft** — The invoice has been created but is not yet sent for payment
- **Pending** — The invoice is awaiting payment
- **Paid** — Payment has been received successfully
- **Payment failed** — The payment attempt did not go through
- **Voided** — The invoice has been canceled and is no longer valid

## Downloading an Invoice PDF

1. Open the invoice you want to download.
2. Select the **Download PDF** option.
3. Mediyn will provide a link to download the invoice as a PDF file.

## Good to Know

- Only therapists and clinic administrators can view invoices.
- Invoices are scoped to your practice. You will only see invoices belonging to your clinic.
- If an invoice shows "Payment failed," check that the patient has a valid payment method on file.
- You can void an invoice if it was created in error. Only clinic administrators can void invoices.
