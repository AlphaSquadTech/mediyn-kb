# Tips for Billing & Payments

## Recommendations

- **Collect payment methods early** — Ask patients to add a payment method when they first register. This avoids delays when it is time to charge for a session.

- **Use auto-invoicing for efficiency** — Turn on automatic invoice creation in your billing settings. Mediyn will generate invoices as soon as sessions are approved, saving you manual work.

- **Pair auto-invoicing with auto-charge** — For the smoothest billing experience, enable both auto-create invoices and auto-charge. Payments will be collected automatically after each approved session.

- **Review failed payments promptly** — Check invoices with a "Payment failed" status regularly. Reach out to the patient to update their payment method before the balance grows.

- **Void invoices instead of ignoring them** — If an invoice was created by mistake, void it rather than leaving it unpaid. This keeps your records clean and accurate.

- **Download PDF invoices for your records** — Keep copies of paid invoices for your accounting. Mediyn lets you download each invoice as a PDF.

- **Keep clinic information up to date** — Your clinic name, address, and contact details appear on every invoice. Make sure they are current in your settings.

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I change the currency for my practice?**
A: Yes. Go to Billing Settings and update the currency field. This applies to all future invoices.

**Q: What happens if a patient does not have a payment method on file?**
A: Mediyn will create the invoice, but the automatic charge will not go through. You will need to add a payment method and process the payment manually.

**Q: Can I undo a voided invoice?**
A: No. Once an invoice is voided, it cannot be reversed. You would need to create a new invoice if needed.

**Q: Who can manage billing settings?**
A: Only clinic administrators can view and change billing settings. Therapists can view invoices but cannot change billing preferences.

**Q: Does changing billing settings affect past invoices?**
A: No. Changes to billing settings only apply to future sessions and invoices.
