# How to Review Clinical Notes

View the transcription, summary, and key insights Mediyn generated for your session.

## Steps

1. Open the session in Mediyn.
2. Navigate to the clinical notes section.
3. Review the list of artifacts available for this session.

Each artifact shows:

- The type (Transcription, Summary, or Key Insights)
- The current state (In Review or Approved)
- Where it was generated (on your device or by Mediyn)
- Who last edited it
- When it was last updated

4. Select any artifact to read the full content.

**What each artifact contains:**

- **Transcription** -- The full text of the session conversation
- **Summary** -- A concise overview of the session
- **Key Insights** -- Organized into three areas:
  - Clinical insights from the session
  - Red flags that may need attention
  - Suggested follow-up questions for future sessions

## What to Expect

After a session is processed, all generated artifacts appear in the clinical notes section. They start in the **In Review** state, ready for you to read through.

You can review them in any order. Take your time to ensure accuracy before approving.

## Good to Know

- Artifacts are available as soon as processing is complete.
- You do not need to review all artifacts at once. Come back any time before approving.
- The source indicator helps you understand whether the notes were generated on your device or by Mediyn. Both are equally editable.
- Key insights highlight important observations automatically. Always review red flags carefully.
