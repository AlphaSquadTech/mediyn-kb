# Dashboard & Search

Your Mediyn dashboard gives you an at-a-glance summary of your day plus a powerful search to find anything fast.

## What You Can Do

- View today's session count and pending tasks in one place
- See how many items need your attention across worksheets, intake packets, assessments, and messages
- Get alerted to assessment results that triggered alert rules
- Check pending AI assessment recommendations
- Search across patients, sessions, therapists, and documents from a single search bar

## Key Concepts

- **Dashboard counters** — Small number badges that show how many items are waiting for your attention. These update automatically as you work.
- **Pending items** — Tasks that need your action, such as worksheet approvals, intake packet reviews, or unread messages.
- **Assessment alerts** — Notifications when a patient's assessment score crosses a threshold you have set. Mediyn shows alerts from the last 30 days.
- **Global search** — A single search bar that looks across all your patients, sessions, therapists, and documents at once. You can narrow results by type.
