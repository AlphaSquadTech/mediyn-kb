# Tips for Dashboard & Search

## Recommendations

- **Start your day on the dashboard** — Check your counters each morning to prioritize your workload.
- **Address assessment alerts first** — These may indicate a patient in distress. Review them before other tasks.
- **Use search to save time** — Instead of navigating through menus, type a patient name in the search bar to jump straight to their record.
- **Narrow search results by type** — If you know you are looking for a patient, select the patients type to filter out everything else.
- **Keep your unread messages low** — Responding to messages promptly improves patient engagement and satisfaction.

## Common Questions

**Q: Why does my dashboard show zero for all counters?**
A: This means you have no pending items right now. All worksheets are reviewed, all intake packets are complete, and all messages are read.

**Q: Can I customize which counters appear on my dashboard?**
A: The dashboard shows all available counters by default. Each counter only appears if the related feature is active in your practice.

**Q: Does the search find archived patients?**
A: Search results include active records. If you need to find an archived patient, check your patient list with the archived filter enabled.

**Q: How quickly do dashboard counters update?**
A: Counters update in real time. When a patient submits a worksheet or sends a message, your counter increases within seconds.
