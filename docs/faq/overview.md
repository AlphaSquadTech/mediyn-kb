# Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about using Mediyn.

![Mediyn Dashboard](../images/clinic/dashboard.png)

## Account & Security

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: On the sign-in screen, select "Forgot password." Enter your email address and Mediyn will send you a reset link. Follow the link to create a new password.

**Q: What is two-step verification?**
A: Two-step verification adds extra security to your account. After entering your password, you confirm your identity with a code from your phone or authenticator app.

**Q: How do I set up two-step verification?**
A: Go to your account settings and find the security section. Select "Enable two-step verification" and follow the on-screen instructions to link your phone or authenticator app.

**Q: What is a trusted device?**
A: A trusted device is a computer or phone you have verified before. Mediyn remembers trusted devices so you do not need to enter a verification code every time you sign in.

**Q: How do I remove a trusted device?**
A: Open your account settings and go to the security section. You will see a list of your trusted devices. Select "Remove" next to any device you no longer use.

**Q: What should I do if I am locked out of my account?**
A: Contact your clinic administrator. They can help restore your access. If you have lost your verification method, the administrator can reset your two-step verification.

## Scheduling

**Q: How do I set a cancellation policy?**
A: Go to your clinic settings and look for the scheduling section. You can set a cancellation window, such as 24 hours. Patients who cancel within that window can be charged a cancellation fee.

**Q: Can I schedule recurring sessions?**
A: Yes. When booking a session, choose the "Recurring" option. Set how often the session should repeat, such as weekly or every two weeks. Mediyn will create all the future sessions for you.

**Q: How do time zones work in Mediyn?**
A: Mediyn displays all times in your local time zone. You set your time zone during onboarding. If your patient is in a different time zone, Mediyn adjusts the session time so both of you see the correct time.

**Q: How do I block off time on my calendar?**
A: Open your availability settings and add a block for the time you need. This prevents anyone from booking a session during that window.

**Q: Can patients book their own sessions?**
A: Yes, if your clinic enables self-booking. Patients can view your available time slots and request a session. You can approve or adjust the booking as needed.

## Sessions & Documentation

**Q: How do I record a session?**
A: Start the session in Mediyn and select the record option. Mediyn will capture the session securely. When the session ends, the recording is saved to the patient's record.

**Q: Can I upload a recording made outside of Mediyn?**
A: Yes. Go to the patient's record and select the option to upload a recording. Choose your file and Mediyn will attach it to the session.

**Q: How do clinical notes work?**
A: After a session, you create a clinical note. You can use a structured template or write in your own format. Notes can be submitted for review and approval by a supervisor or colleague.

**Q: What is the approval flow for clinical notes?**
A: When you submit a note, it goes to a designated reviewer. The reviewer can approve the note, request changes, or add comments. You will be notified of the outcome.

**Q: Can I assign worksheets to a patient?**
A: Yes. Browse the worksheet library, select a worksheet, and assign it to your patient. The patient can complete it through their portal. You will see their responses in their record.

**Q: How do I use assessments?**
A: Go to the patient's record and select "Assessments." Choose an assessment tool, assign it, and the patient can complete it. Mediyn scores the results and tracks changes over time.

## Billing & Payments

**Q: What subscription plans does Mediyn offer?**
A: Mediyn offers several plans based on your clinic's size and needs. Visit the Plans and Pricing section of this guide for full details.

**Q: How do I create an invoice?**
A: Go to the patient's billing section and select "Create invoice." Add the services provided, and Mediyn will generate a professional invoice. You can send it to the patient directly.

**Q: What are session packages?**
A: Session packages let you bundle multiple sessions at a set price. For example, you might offer ten sessions for a discounted rate. Patients purchase the package and sessions are deducted as they are used.

**Q: How do superbills work?**
A: A superbill is a detailed receipt your patient can submit to their insurance company for reimbursement. Mediyn generates superbills with all the required information, including service codes and provider details.

**Q: How do patients pay?**
A: Patients can pay by credit or debit card through Mediyn. You can send invoices with a payment link, or charge a card on file. Mediyn handles payment processing securely.

**Q: How do I track outstanding balances?**
A: Check the billing section of your dashboard. You will see a summary of unpaid invoices and overdue balances. You can send payment reminders to patients from there.

## Privacy & Compliance

**Q: Who can see my patient data?**
A: Only authorized members of your clinic can access patient records. You control permissions through your clinic's admin settings. Patient data is never shared with other clinics.

**Q: What are audit logs?**
A: Audit logs track important actions in your clinic's account. They record who viewed, changed, or accessed records and when. This helps you stay accountable and meet compliance requirements.

**Q: Can I redact information from a record?**
A: Yes. Mediyn supports redaction for sensitive information. Redacted content is permanently removed and cannot be recovered. Use this feature carefully and in line with your clinic's policies.

**Q: How does Mediyn protect patient privacy?**
A: Mediyn uses strong security measures to protect your data. All information is stored securely, and access is controlled by your clinic's permission settings. Mediyn follows industry standards for health data protection.

**Q: Can patients request access to their data?**
A: Yes. Patients can request a copy of their records through the patient portal or by contacting your clinic. Your clinic administrator can manage these requests.

## Patient Access

**Q: What is the patient portal?**
A: The patient portal is a secure area where your patients can view their appointments, complete forms, check messages, and make payments. Each patient gets their own private access.

**Q: How do patients sign in?**
A: Patients receive a secure sign-in link by email. They click the link to access their portal without needing to remember a password. A new link is sent each time they need to sign in.

**Q: Can patients complete intake forms online?**
A: Yes. You can send intake forms to patients before their first visit. Patients fill out the forms on their device, and the information appears in their record automatically.

**Q: Can patients see their session notes?**
A: This depends on your clinic's settings. You can choose whether patients can view their clinical notes through the portal. Some clinics share approved notes, while others keep them private.

**Q: How do patients book appointments?**
A: If self-booking is enabled, patients can view available times and request a session through their portal. You will receive a notification to confirm the booking.
