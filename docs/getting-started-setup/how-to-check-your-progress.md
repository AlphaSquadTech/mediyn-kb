# How to Check Your Onboarding Progress

See where you are in the Mediyn setup process and find out what steps remain.

## Steps

1. Sign in to your Mediyn account.
2. Navigate to the onboarding section. Mediyn may also show a progress indicator on your dashboard.
3. View your current progress.

## What to Expect

You will see the following information:

- **Workflow type** — Whether you are completing clinic setup or therapist setup.
- **Status** — Your current stage:
  - **Not started** — Setup has not begun.
  - **In progress** — Some steps are done, but more remain.
  - **Complete** — All required steps are finished.
- **Completed steps** — A list of the steps you have already finished.
- **Next step** — The step Mediyn recommends you complete next.
- **Resumable** — Whether you can pick up where you left off. In most cases, yes.
- **Plan details** — Your current subscription plan for context.

## Good to Know

- You can check your progress at any time, even after completing onboarding.
- If your onboarding is resumable, Mediyn takes you directly to the next incomplete step.
- Completing all steps unlocks the full set of Mediyn features for your account.
