# How to Complete Clinic Onboarding

Set up your clinic workspace in Mediyn so your team can start managing patients and appointments.

![Dashboard](../images/clinic/dashboard.png)

## Steps

1. Sign in to your clinic admin account.
2. Mediyn will launch the onboarding guide automatically.
3. You'll need to provide:
   - **Clinic name** — The name of your practice as you want it displayed.
   - **Time zone** — Your clinic's local time zone. This affects scheduling and appointment times.
   - **Default session duration** — The standard length of appointments in minutes (for example, 50 or 60 minutes).
4. You can also:
   - **Set scheduling rules** — Configure preferences for how appointments are booked across your clinic.
5. Complete each step and move to the next.

## What to Expect

- After completing setup, your clinic workspace is created and ready to use.
- Your clinic will be in **Active** status.
- Mediyn shows you the next step if there are more items to configure.
- You can leave and come back at any time. Mediyn saves your progress.

## Good to Know

- The default session duration applies to new appointments. Individual therapists can set their own preferences later.
- Your clinic operates in one of two modes:
  - **Clinic** — A multi-therapist practice with shared management.
  - **Therapist** — A solo practice for an individual therapist.
- You can update your clinic name, time zone, and other settings after onboarding.
