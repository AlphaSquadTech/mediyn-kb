# How to Invite Team Members

Add therapists to your clinic by sending email invitations through Mediyn.

## Sending an Invitation

### Steps

1. Sign in to your Mediyn clinic admin account.
2. Go to the team or invitations section.
3. Select **Invite Therapist** (or a similar option).
4. You'll need to provide:
   - **Email address** — The therapist's email where the invitation will be sent.
   - **Role** — Currently, team members are invited as **Therapist**.
5. Select **Send Invitation**.

### What to Expect

- The therapist receives an email with a link to join your clinic.
- The invitation appears in your invitations list with its current status.

## Viewing Your Invitations

### Steps

1. Go to the team or invitations section.
2. View the list of all invitations you have sent.

### What to Expect

- You will see all pending and completed invitations for your clinic.
- Each entry shows the therapist's email and invitation status.

## Accepting an Invitation (For Invited Therapists)

### Steps

1. Open the invitation email and select the join link.
2. You'll need to provide:
   - **First name** and **Last name**.
   - **Password** — Must include an uppercase letter, a lowercase letter, a number, and a special character.
3. Select **Accept Invitation**.

### What to Expect

- Your account is created and linked to the clinic.
- You are automatically signed in.
- Mediyn guides you through therapist onboarding to complete your profile.

## Good to Know

- Only clinic administrators can send invitations.
- Each invitation is tied to a specific email address. The therapist must use that email to accept.
- If an invitation expires or the therapist does not receive it, you can send a new one.
- Invited therapists do not need to create a separate account. The invitation link handles account creation.
