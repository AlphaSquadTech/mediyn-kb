# Getting Started & Setup

Mediyn guides you through setting up your clinic or therapist profile so you can start seeing patients quickly.

![Dashboard](../images/clinic/dashboard.png)

## What You Can Do

- Set up your clinic workspace with default settings
- Complete your therapist profile and schedule preferences
- Check your onboarding progress at any time
- Pick up where you left off if you step away during setup
- Invite therapists to join your clinic

## Key Concepts

- **Onboarding** — The guided setup process you complete after creating your account. It walks you through essential configuration steps.
- **Clinic workspace** — Your shared practice environment where therapists, patients, and schedules are managed.
- **Onboarding progress** — Mediyn tracks which setup steps you have completed. You can return and pick up where you left off.
- **Invitation** — An email sent to a therapist inviting them to join your clinic. They create their own account using the invitation link.
