# Tips for Getting Started & Setup

## Recommendations

- **Complete onboarding in one sitting if possible** — While Mediyn saves your progress, finishing setup in one go gets your clinic up and running faster.
- **Set your time zone correctly** — This affects all appointment times and notifications. Double-check it during setup.
- **Configure default session duration early** — This saves time when creating appointments later. Individual therapists can still set their own preferences.
- **Invite your team right away** — The sooner therapists join, the sooner they can set up their profiles and availability.
- **Set up availability templates during onboarding** — Defining your weekly schedule now means Mediyn can help with scheduling immediately.

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I redo a setup step after completing onboarding?**
A: Yes. You can update your clinic settings and therapist profile at any time from the settings area.

**Q: What if I close the browser during onboarding?**
A: Mediyn saves your progress. When you sign in again, you will pick up where you left off.

**Q: Do invited therapists need to complete onboarding too?**
A: Yes. Each therapist completes their own profile setup after accepting the invitation.

**Q: Can I invite someone who is not a therapist?**
A: Currently, invitations are for therapist roles. Contact Mediyn support for other team member types.

**Q: What subscription plan do invited therapists get?**
A: Invited therapists join under your clinic's existing plan. They do not need their own subscription.
