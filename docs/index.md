# Welcome & Getting Started

Mediyn is your all-in-one platform for running a therapy or mental health practice.

![Mediyn Dashboard](images/clinic/dashboard.png)

## Who Mediyn Is For

Mediyn is built for the people who power therapy practices every day.

- **Therapists and counselors** who see patients, write clinical notes, and manage sessions
- **Clinic administrators** who handle scheduling, billing, and day-to-day operations
- **Support staff** who coordinate intake, manage patient records, and assist with documentation

Whether you work solo or as part of a larger clinic, Mediyn fits your workflow.

## What You Can Do with Mediyn

Mediyn brings together everything you need in one place. Here is a quick look at what the platform covers.

- **Patient Management** -- Keep all your patient information organized and easy to find. Track contact details, session history, and clinical records.
- **Scheduling** -- Set your availability, book sessions, and manage your calendar. Handle cancellations and recurring appointments with ease.
- **Session Recording** -- Record therapy sessions securely. Recordings are stored safely and linked to each patient's record.
- **Clinical Notes** -- Write, review, and approve session notes. Use structured templates or free-form entries to document your work.
- **Worksheets** -- Assign therapeutic worksheets and handouts to your patients. Track completion and review responses.
- **Assessments** -- Use clinical assessments to measure progress over time. View scores and trends for each patient.
- **Messaging** -- Communicate securely with your patients between sessions. Keep all conversations in one place.
- **Billing** -- Create invoices, manage subscription plans, and accept payments. Track outstanding balances and payment history.
- **Intake Forms** -- Collect patient information before the first visit. Customize your forms to gather what matters most.
- **Compliance** -- Stay on top of privacy and documentation requirements. Mediyn includes audit logs, consent tracking, and data access controls.
