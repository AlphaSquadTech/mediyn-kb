# How to Create Intake Packets

Send a bundle of intake forms and consent documents to your patient in one step.

## Steps

1. Navigate to your patient's profile in Mediyn.
2. Select the option to create a new intake packet.
3. Choose which form templates to include in the packet. You can combine intake questionnaires, consent forms, and other documents into a single packet.
4. Set the expiration window (in days). This controls how long the patient has to complete the forms.
5. Send the packet to your patient.

**You'll need to provide:**

- The patient you are sending the packet to
- One or more form templates to include
- An expiration window (number of days)

## What to Expect

After you create the packet, Mediyn assigns it a reference number and sets the status to **Sent**. Your patient receives the packet and can begin filling out forms right away.

The packet moves through these stages:

- **Not sent** -- Created but not yet delivered
- **Sent** -- Delivered to the patient
- **Partially completed** -- The patient has finished some forms but not all
- **Completed** -- All forms are submitted
- **Expired** -- The patient did not finish before the deadline

You can check the status of any packet from the patient's profile.

## Managing Form Templates

Before you can create packets, you need form templates. Here is how to work with them.

**Viewing available templates**

Mediyn provides a library of system templates and any custom templates your practice has created. You can narrow the list by:

- Status (Draft, Published, or Archived)
- Form type (Intake, HIPAA consent, Treatment consent, Telehealth consent, Custom consent, or Good Faith Estimate)
- Category (HIPAA, Treatment consent, Telehealth consent, Demographics, Insurance, Emergency contact, Mental health history, or Custom)
- System templates vs. your own custom templates

**Creating a custom template**

1. Go to your form template settings.
2. Select the option to create a new template.
3. Give it a title and choose the form type.
4. Add your form fields.
5. Choose a category to keep things organized.
6. Indicate whether this form requires a patient signature.

You can also provide an optional description.

**Cloning a template**

Want to start from an existing template? You can clone any template -- including system templates -- to create your own editable copy. The clone starts as a Draft, and you can optionally give it a new title.

**Editing a template**

You can update the title, fields, description, category, status, and signature requirement of any template you own. System templates are read-only. To customize a system template, clone it first.

**Archiving a template**

When you no longer need a template, you can archive it. Templates that are already included in active intake packets cannot be archived until those packets are resolved.

## Good to Know

- Only published templates can be included in intake packets.
- System templates are ready to use but cannot be edited. Clone them to make changes.
- Expired packets can be resent by creating a new packet for the same patient.
