# How to Track Consent

Monitor which consent forms each patient has signed, and collect new signatures electronically.

![Patient Intake](../images/clinic/patient-detail-intake.png)

## Steps

### For patients completing consent forms

1. The patient opens their pending intake forms in Mediyn.
2. They review the consent document.
3. They fill in any required fields.
4. They sign using one of two methods:
   - **Checkbox** -- Check a box to confirm agreement
   - **Drawn** -- Draw a signature on screen
5. They submit the signed consent form.

Patients can save their progress and come back later. The form stays in progress until they submit it.

### For therapists and staff reviewing consent status

1. Open the patient's profile in Mediyn.
2. Navigate to the consent section to see all consent records.
3. Review the status of each consent type.

## What to Expect

Each consent form response has one of these stages:

- **In progress** -- The patient has started but not yet submitted
- **Submitted** -- The patient has signed and submitted the form

Once a consent form is submitted, Mediyn records the signature along with details about when and how it was signed. This creates a clear record for your files.

You can view all of a patient's consent statuses in one place. This includes HIPAA, treatment, telehealth, and any custom consent forms.

## Good to Know

- Mediyn automatically captures details about the signing process for your records.
- Patients can save a partially completed consent form and return to finish it later.
- Each consent type is tracked separately. A patient may have signed one consent but not another.
- You can include multiple consent forms in a single intake packet.
