# Intake Forms & Consent

Mediyn helps you collect patient information, track signed consents, and deliver Good Faith Estimates -- all digitally.

## What You Can Do

- Create and send intake form packets to patients before their first visit
- Track consent signatures for HIPAA, treatment, telehealth, and custom agreements
- Generate and send Good Faith Estimates as required by the No Surprises Act
- Review completed intake responses at any time
- Use built-in templates or create your own custom forms
- Clone and customize system-provided templates to fit your practice

## Key Concepts

**Intake Packet**
A bundle of forms sent to a patient. A packet can include intake questionnaires, consent forms, and Good Faith Estimates. Each packet has an expiration window you set when you create it.

**Form Template**
The reusable design behind each form. Mediyn provides system templates you can use as-is, or you can clone them to create your own versions. Templates go through three stages: Draft, Published, and Archived.

**Consent Form**
A specific type of form that requires a patient signature. Mediyn supports these consent types:

- **HIPAA consent** -- Privacy practices acknowledgment
- **Treatment consent** -- Agreement to receive care
- **Telehealth consent** -- Agreement for remote sessions
- **Custom consent** -- Any other agreement your practice needs

**Good Faith Estimate (GFE)**
A cost estimate you provide to uninsured or self-pay patients. This is required under the No Surprises Act. The GFE includes provider details, diagnosis codes, planned services, and expected costs.

**E-Signature**
Patients can sign consent forms electronically. Mediyn supports two signature methods:

- **Checkbox** -- The patient checks a box to confirm agreement
- **Drawn** -- The patient draws their signature on screen

**Form Categories**
When creating templates, you can organize them by category:

- HIPAA
- Treatment consent
- Telehealth consent
- Demographics
- Insurance
- Emergency contact
- Mental health history
- Custom
