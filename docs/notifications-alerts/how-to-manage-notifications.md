# How to Manage Notifications

Mediyn sends you notifications whenever something needs your attention. Here is how to stay on top of them.

## Steps

1. Click the notification icon in Mediyn to open your notification feed.
2. Browse your notifications. The most recent ones appear at the top.
3. To see only notifications you have not read yet, turn on the "Unread only" option.
4. Click on any notification to view the related item.
5. To mark a notification as read, select it and confirm. Mediyn updates the notification status immediately.

## What to Expect

After you mark a notification as read, it no longer appears in your unread list. It remains in your full notification feed for future reference.

## Good to Know

- Notifications cover a wide range of events, including new patient messages, submitted worksheets, assessment completions, and more.
- You can filter your feed to show only unread items. This helps you focus on what still needs attention.
- Marking a notification as read does not remove it. You can always go back and review it later.
- Patients also have their own notification feed within the patient portal.
