# Tips for Notifications & Live Updates

## Recommendations

- **Check notifications regularly** — Make it a habit to review your notification feed at least a few times per day.
- **Use the unread filter** — When your feed gets long, switch to unread-only mode to focus on what is new.
- **Act on notifications promptly** — Responding quickly to patient messages and worksheet submissions improves care quality.
- **Keep Mediyn open during your workday** — Live updates only arrive when the app is active. Keeping it open ensures you do not miss anything.
- **Mark notifications as read when done** — This keeps your unread count accurate and helps you track what still needs attention.

## Common Questions

**Q: Will I get notifications for every patient in my practice?**
A: You receive notifications only for patients and events that are relevant to you based on your role and caseload.

**Q: What happens if I miss a live update?**
A: Missed updates are captured in your notification feed. Check your notifications to catch up on anything you may have missed.

**Q: Do live updates work on mobile devices?**
A: Yes. Live updates work in any browser or the Mediyn mobile app.

**Q: Can I turn off notifications?**
A: Notification settings depend on your practice configuration. Contact your practice administrator to adjust notification preferences.

**Q: How far back does my notification feed go?**
A: Your notification feed retains all notifications. You can scroll back to review older items at any time.
