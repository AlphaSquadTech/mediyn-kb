# How Patients Sign In

Mediyn uses passwordless sign-in so patients never need to remember a password. Here is how the process works.

## First-Time Access: Invitation from the Therapist

### Steps

1. Open the patient's record in Mediyn.
2. Choose the option to send a portal invitation.
3. Confirm the patient's email address. Mediyn sends a magic link to that email.
4. The patient opens their email and clicks the magic link.
5. Mediyn verifies the link and signs the patient in. The patient chooses their device platform (iPhone, Android, or web browser).
6. The device is registered as a trusted device for future visits.

### What to Expect

After clicking the magic link, the patient lands in the Mediyn patient portal. Their invitation status changes from "Pending" to "Accepted." Their app access status changes to "Active."

## Returning Access: Requesting a New Magic Link

If a patient needs to sign in again on a new device or after their session has expired:

### Steps

1. The patient goes to the Mediyn sign-in page.
2. They enter their email address.
3. Mediyn sends a new magic link to their email.
4. The patient clicks the link and is signed in.

### What to Expect

The patient is signed in and taken to the portal. If they use a new device, it is added to their trusted device list.

## Good to Know

- Magic links expire after a set time. If a patient waits too long, they can request a new one.
- Each magic link can only be used once. After clicking it, the link becomes inactive.
- Patients do not need to create a username or password at any point.
- You'll need to provide the patient's email address when sending an invitation.
- Patients can sign in from an iPhone, Android device, or web browser.
