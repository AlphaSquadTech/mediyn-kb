# How Patients Use Biometrics

Patients can set up fingerprint or face recognition on their mobile device for faster sign-in to Mediyn.

![Patient Login](../images/patient/patient-login.png)

## Steps

1. The patient signs in to Mediyn using a magic link on their mobile device.
2. During sign-in, Mediyn detects the device and registers it as a trusted device.
3. The patient's device platform is recorded (iPhone or Android).
4. On future visits, the patient can use their device's built-in fingerprint scanner or face recognition to sign in.

## What to Expect

Once biometrics are set up, the patient skips the magic link step on that device. They simply open Mediyn and use their fingerprint or face to sign in. This works until the device is removed from the trusted list.

## Good to Know

- Biometric sign-in is available on iPhones (Face ID / Touch ID) and Android devices (fingerprint / face unlock).
- The biometric data stays on the patient's device. Mediyn never stores fingerprint or facial data.
- If a patient gets a new phone, they will need to sign in with a magic link once to register the new device.
- Biometric sign-in only works on devices that are in the patient's trusted device list.
- Web browsers do not support biometric sign-in. Patients using a computer sign in with magic links.
