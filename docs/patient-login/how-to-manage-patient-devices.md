# How to Manage Patient Devices

You can view and manage the devices a patient uses to access Mediyn. This helps you maintain security and control access.

## Viewing Trusted Devices

### Steps

1. Open the patient's record in Mediyn.
2. Go to the devices section.
3. Review the list of devices the patient has used to sign in.

### What to Expect

You will see a list of all trusted devices for that patient. Each entry shows the device name and platform.

## Revoking a Device

If a patient loses a device or you need to remove access for security reasons:

### Steps

1. Open the patient's record in Mediyn.
2. Go to the devices section.
3. Find the device you want to remove.
4. Choose the option to revoke the device.
5. Confirm the action.

### What to Expect

The device is immediately removed from the patient's trusted list. Any active sessions on that device are ended. The patient will need to sign in again with a new magic link if they want to use that device in the future.

## Good to Know

- Revoking a device does not delete the patient's account. It only removes that specific device's access.
- The patient can always request a new magic link to sign in on a different device.
- Review patient devices periodically if you have security concerns.
- If a patient reports a lost or stolen phone, revoke that device right away.
