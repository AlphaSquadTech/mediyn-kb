# How to Archive and Restore Patients

Archive patient records you no longer need in your active list, and restore them if circumstances change.

![Patients List](../images/clinic/patients.png)

## Archiving a Patient

### Steps

1. Sign in to your Mediyn clinic admin account.
2. Navigate to the **Patients** section.
3. Open the patient record you want to archive.
4. Select **Archive** (or a similar option).
5. Confirm the action.

### What to Expect

- The patient's status changes to **Archived**.
- The record is hidden from your default patient list.
- No data is deleted. The record is preserved for your records.

## Restoring an Archived Patient

### Steps

1. Navigate to the **Patients** section.
2. Use the status option to show **Archived** patients.
3. Open the archived patient's record.
4. Select **Restore** (or a similar option).
5. Confirm the action.

### What to Expect

- The patient's status changes back to **Active**.
- The record reappears in your default patient list.
- All previous data, notes, and history are intact.

## Good to Know

- Only clinic administrators can archive and restore patient records.
- Archiving is not the same as deleting. All information is preserved.
- You can view archived patients at any time by changing your list view to show archived records.
- If a former patient returns for care, restoring their record saves you from creating a duplicate.
