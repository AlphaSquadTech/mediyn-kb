# How to Invite Patients to the Mediyn Portal

Give patients access to the Mediyn patient portal so they can manage their own appointments and information.

## Sending a Portal Invitation When Creating a Patient

### Steps

1. While adding a new patient, enter the patient's **email address**.
2. Turn on the **Send portal invitation** option.
3. Save the patient record.

### What to Expect

- The patient receives an email with a secure sign-in link.
- The patient's app status changes to **Invited**.
- Once the patient opens the link and signs in, their status changes to **Active**.

## Understanding Patient App Status

A patient's portal access moves through these stages:

- **Not invited** — No invitation has been sent yet.
- **Invited** — The invitation was sent. The patient has not signed in yet.
- **Active** — The patient has accepted the invitation and is using the portal.
- **Deactivated** — Portal access has been removed.

## Good to Know

- You need the patient's email address to send a portal invitation.
- You can send the invitation at the time of creating the patient record. If you skip it, you can add the email and send it later.
- The patient portal lets patients view their upcoming appointments and complete assigned forms.
- If a patient's portal access is deactivated, they can no longer sign in. You may need to contact Mediyn support to reactivate access.
- You can also choose to send intake forms at the same time as the portal invitation. This is a convenient way to collect paperwork before the first appointment.
