# How to View and Update Patients

Find patients in Mediyn, review their details, and edit their records as needed.

## Viewing Your Patient List

### Steps

1. Sign in to your Mediyn account.
2. Navigate to the **Patients** section.
3. Browse the list of patients.

### What to Expect

- You will see a list of patients with their basic details.
- Results are shown in pages. Use the navigation controls to load more.
- You can narrow down the list by:
  - **Status** — Show only **Active** or **Archived** patients.
  - **Therapist** — Show only patients assigned to a specific therapist.

### Good to Know

- Therapists see only patients assigned to them.
- Clinic administrators can view all patients in the clinic.

## Viewing a Patient's Full Details

### Steps

1. From the patient list, select a patient's name.
2. View their complete profile.

### What to Expect

You will see the following information:

- **Name** — First name, last name, preferred name, and display name.
- **Status** — Active or Archived.
- **Consent status** — Pending, Obtained, or Revoked.
- **Contact information** — Phone, email, and address.
- **Date of birth**.
- **Insurance information** (if available).
- **Risk flags** — Any clinical considerations flagged for this patient.
- **App status** — Whether the patient has been invited to or is using the Mediyn portal.
- **Intake status** — Progress on intake paperwork.
- **Assigned therapist**.

### Detailed Patient View

For a more complete summary, you can open the detailed patient view. This includes everything above, plus:

- **Join date** — When the patient record was created.
- **Last session date** — When their most recent appointment took place.
- **Next scheduled session** — Their upcoming appointment, if any.
- **Total sessions** — The number of sessions on record.
- **Attachments count** — How many files are attached to the patient record.
- **Worksheet assignments** — A summary of assigned worksheets.
- **Consent summary** — An overview of consent forms.
- **Assessment summary** — An overview of assigned assessments.
- **Latest scores** — The most recent score for each assessment type.
- **Assessment alerts** — The number of assessment responses that triggered alerts.
- **Session history** — A page-by-page list of past sessions with clinical summaries.

## Updating a Patient Record

### Steps

1. Open the patient's profile.
2. Select **Edit** or a similar option.
3. You can update any of the following:
   - **First name**, **Last name**, and **Preferred name**.
   - **Consent status** — Change to Pending, Obtained, or Revoked.
   - **Risk flags** — Add or remove clinical risk indicators.
   - **Date of birth**.
   - **Phone number**.
   - **Address** — Street address, city, state, and zip code.
   - **Email address**.
4. Save your changes.

### What to Expect

- The updated record is saved immediately.
- The display name updates automatically if you change the preferred name or legal name.

## Good to Know

- Both therapists and clinic administrators can update patient records.
- Therapists can only update patients assigned to them.
- The display name and app status cannot be set manually. They are managed by Mediyn.
