# How to Choose a Plan

When you are ready to subscribe, Mediyn walks you through selecting a plan and completing payment.

## Steps

1. Sign in to Mediyn.
2. Go to your billing or subscription settings.
3. Choose the plan you want to subscribe to.
4. If you are subscribing to a clinic plan, select the number of therapist seats you need.
5. Mediyn takes you to a secure checkout page to enter your payment details.
6. Complete the checkout process.

## What You'll Need to Provide

- The plan you want to subscribe to
- The number of therapist seats (clinic plans only)
- Your payment card details

## What to Expect

After checkout, your subscription activates immediately. You are taken back to Mediyn where you can start using all the features included in your plan. You will also receive a confirmation of your new subscription.

## Good to Know

- Some plans include a free trial period. During the trial, you can explore all features before being charged.
- You can change your plan later if your needs change. See the guide on managing your subscription.
- Mediyn uses secure payment processing. Your card information is handled safely and is not stored on Mediyn servers.
