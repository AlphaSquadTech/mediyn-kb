# How to Manage Your Subscription

You can upgrade, downgrade, adjust seats, cancel, or reactivate your Mediyn subscription at any time.

![Billing Settings](../images/clinic/settings-billing.png)

## Changing Your Plan

### Steps

1. Sign in to Mediyn and go to your billing settings.
2. Choose the option to change your plan.
3. Select the new plan you want to switch to.
4. Review the cost preview. Mediyn shows you the adjusted amount for the current billing period.
5. Confirm the change.

### What to Expect

Your plan changes take effect right away. If you upgraded, new features are available immediately. If you downgraded, you keep access to your current features until the end of the billing period. Mediyn adjusts your next invoice to reflect the prorated cost.

## Adjusting Therapist Seats (Clinic Plans)

### Steps

1. Go to your billing settings.
2. Choose the option to update therapist seats.
3. Enter the new number of seats you need.
4. Review the cost preview.
5. Confirm the change.

### What to Expect

The seat count updates immediately. Your next invoice reflects the prorated cost of the seat change.

## Previewing Cost Changes

Before you change your plan or adjust seats, you can preview the cost impact.

### Steps

1. Go to your billing settings.
2. Choose the change you want to make (new plan or new seat count).
3. Review the cost preview. It shows the adjusted amount due, the effective date, and a line-by-line breakdown.

### What to Expect

The preview shows what your next charge will be. This includes credits for unused time on your current plan and charges for the new plan.

## Canceling Your Subscription

### Steps

1. Go to your billing settings.
2. Choose the option to cancel your subscription.
3. Confirm the cancellation.

### What to Expect

- If you are on a free trial, cancellation takes effect immediately with no charge.
- If you have an active paid subscription, cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You keep full access until then.

## Reactivating a Canceled Subscription

If you canceled but changed your mind before the end of the billing period:

### Steps

1. Go to your billing settings.
2. Choose the option to reactivate your subscription.
3. Confirm reactivation.

### What to Expect

Your subscription continues as if it was never canceled. No additional charges apply until your next regular billing date.

## Managing Your Payment Method

### Adding or Replacing Your Card

1. Go to your billing settings.
2. Choose the option to update your payment method.
3. Enter your new card details on the secure form.
4. Submit the form.

Your new card becomes the default payment method for your subscription.

### Viewing Your Current Card

Go to your billing settings to see your card on file. Mediyn shows the card brand, last four digits, and expiration date.

### Removing Your Card

1. Go to your billing settings.
2. Choose the option to remove your payment method.
3. Confirm the removal.

## Viewing Subscription Invoices

### Steps

1. Go to your billing settings.
2. Open the invoices section.
3. Browse your invoice history. Use the next page of results to see older invoices.
4. Click any invoice to see its full details, including amount, billing period, and status.

### Invoice Statuses

Your invoices move through these stages:

- **Draft** — The invoice is being prepared and has not been finalized yet.
- **Paid** — Payment was successful.
- **Payment failed** — The charge did not go through. Update your payment method.
- **Voided** — The invoice was canceled and no payment is owed.

## Good to Know

- Plan changes and seat adjustments are prorated. You only pay for what you use.
- You can preview the cost of any change before committing.
- Canceling during a trial incurs no charges.
- If your payment fails, your subscription moves to "past due" status. Update your card to restore access.
- Reactivation is only available before the end of the current billing period.
