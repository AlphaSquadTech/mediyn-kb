# Plans & Pricing

Mediyn offers flexible subscription plans so you can choose the right fit for your solo practice or multi-therapist clinic.

![Billing Settings](../images/clinic/settings-billing.png)

## What You Can Do

- Browse all available Mediyn plans and compare features
- Subscribe to a plan that fits your needs
- Upgrade, downgrade, or change your plan at any time
- Adjust the number of therapist seats on clinic plans
- Preview cost changes before committing
- Cancel or reactivate your subscription
- View your subscription invoices and payment history
- Manage your payment method on file

## Key Concepts

- **Plan** — A subscription tier with specific features and pricing. Mediyn offers plans for individual therapists and for clinics with multiple therapists.
- **Therapist seats** — On clinic plans, each therapist who uses Mediyn counts as one seat. You choose how many seats you need.
- **Billing period** — The regular interval at which you are charged. When you change plans mid-period, Mediyn calculates the adjusted cost automatically.
- **Proration** — When you upgrade, downgrade, or change seats mid-billing period, Mediyn adjusts your next bill to reflect only the time on each plan.
- **Subscription status** — Your subscription moves through these stages:
  - **Trialing** — You are in a free trial period. You can explore all features before being charged.
  - **Active** — Your subscription is paid and current. All features are available.
  - **Past due** — A payment attempt failed. Update your payment method to restore full access.
  - **Canceled** — Your subscription has been ended. Access continues until the end of the current billing period (for active plans) or stops immediately (for trial plans).
  - **Incomplete** — Your initial payment has not been processed yet. Complete the payment to activate your plan.
