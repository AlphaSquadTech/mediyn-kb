# Tips for Plans & Pricing

## Recommendations

- **Start with a trial** — If available, use the free trial to explore Mediyn before choosing a paid plan.
- **Choose the right plan size** — Solo therapists should pick an individual plan. Multi-therapist practices should choose a clinic plan with the right number of seats.
- **Preview before you change** — Always preview the cost impact before switching plans or adjusting seats. This avoids billing surprises.
- **Keep your payment method up to date** — Replace expired cards before your next billing date to avoid going past due.
- **Review your invoices regularly** — Check your billing settings periodically to make sure charges match your expectations.
- **Adjust seats as your team changes** — Add seats when you bring on a new therapist and remove seats when someone leaves. Charges adjust automatically.
- **Reactivate before the period ends** — If you cancel by mistake, you can undo it before the billing period runs out.

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I try Mediyn before paying?**
A: Yes. Some plans offer a free trial. You get full access during the trial with no charge.

**Q: What happens when my trial ends?**
A: If you provided a payment method, your subscription converts to a paid plan automatically. If not, your access may be paused until you subscribe.

**Q: Will I lose my data if I cancel?**
A: No. Your patient records, session history, and documents are preserved. If you resubscribe, everything is still there.

**Q: How does proration work?**
A: When you change plans mid-billing period, Mediyn credits you for unused time on the old plan and charges for time on the new plan. You see the adjusted amount on your next invoice.

**Q: Can I downgrade my plan?**
A: Yes. You can switch to a lower-tier plan at any time. The change applies at the end of your current billing period so you keep access to your current features until then.

**Q: What if my payment fails?**
A: Your subscription moves to "past due" status. Update your payment method in billing settings and Mediyn will retry the charge.

**Q: How do I add more therapist seats?**
A: Go to billing settings and update your seat count. The change takes effect immediately and your next bill is prorated.
