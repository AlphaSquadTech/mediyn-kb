# Understanding Subscription Status

Your Mediyn subscription has a status that tells you where it stands. Here is what each status means.

## Subscription Stages

Your subscription moves through these stages based on your actions and payment history:

- **Trialing** — You are in a free trial period. All features are available. You will not be charged until the trial ends. If you cancel during the trial, it ends immediately with no charge.

- **Active** — Your subscription is paid and current. Everything works normally. Your card is charged at the start of each billing period.

- **Past due** — Your most recent payment did not go through. This usually means your card was declined or expired. Update your payment method in billing settings to restore your subscription to active status.

- **Canceled** — Your subscription has been ended. If you canceled an active subscription, you keep access until the end of the current billing period. If you canceled a trial, access ends immediately. You can reactivate before the period ends.

- **Incomplete** — Your first payment has not been processed yet. This can happen if checkout was interrupted. Return to billing settings to complete the payment and activate your plan.

## What to Do If Your Status Changes Unexpectedly

**Past due:**
1. Go to your billing settings.
2. Check your payment method on file.
3. Update your card if it has expired or was declined.
4. Mediyn will retry the payment automatically.

**Incomplete:**
1. Go to your billing settings.
2. Complete the checkout process that was started earlier.

**Canceled (and you want to continue):**
1. If you are still within your current billing period, go to billing settings and choose reactivate.
2. If your billing period has already ended, you will need to subscribe to a new plan.

## Good to Know

- Mediyn does not delete your data when a subscription is canceled. If you resubscribe later, your records are still available.
- During a trial, you have full access to all plan features so you can evaluate Mediyn before committing.
- If you are past due, resolving the payment issue restores your subscription without losing any data.
