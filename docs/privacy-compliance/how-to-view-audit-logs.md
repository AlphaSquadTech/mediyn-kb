# How to View Audit Logs

Mediyn maintains an unchangeable audit trail so you can review every significant action taken in your practice.

## Steps

1. Go to **Settings** or **Compliance** in Mediyn.
2. Select **Audit Log** or **Audit Events**.
3. Review the list of recorded actions.

## Narrowing Down Your Results

You can narrow down the audit log using these options:

- **Team member** — Show actions performed by a specific person
- **Record type** — Show actions related to a specific kind of record (for example, sessions, patients, invoices)
- **Date range** — Show actions within a start and end date

## What You'll See

Each audit entry includes details about:
- Who performed the action
- What type of record was affected
- When the action occurred

## What to Expect

The audit log is read-only and cannot be changed or deleted. This ensures a trustworthy record for compliance purposes.

## Good to Know

- Audit logs are available to clinic administrators.
- The log captures actions across your entire practice, not just your own.
- Use date range options to focus on a specific time period during audits or reviews.
- Audit logs are essential for demonstrating compliance with privacy regulations. Review them regularly.
- If you notice unexpected activity, investigate promptly and update access permissions if needed.
