# Privacy & Compliance

Mediyn provides built-in privacy controls, audit trails, and compliance features to help you protect patient data and meet regulatory requirements.

## What You Can Do

- View a complete audit trail of actions taken in your practice
- Set up and manage data redaction policies
- Configure security policies for your practice
- Manage protected health information (PHI) visibility rules
- Run periodic access reviews to verify who has access to what
- Update consent status for sessions

## Key Concepts

**Audit trail** — An unchangeable record of every significant action in your practice. Mediyn logs who did what, when, and to which record.

**Redaction policy** — Rules that control how sensitive data is masked or hidden. These policies determine what information is redacted before it reaches certain users or devices.

**PHI masking** — Role-based rules that control which fields are visible to different team members. This ensures staff only see the patient information they need.

**Security policies** — Practice-wide settings that control session timeouts and re-verification requirements:
- **Inactivity timeout** — How long before Mediyn locks your screen after no activity (default: 30 minutes)
- **Maximum session duration** — How long before Mediyn requires you to sign in again, regardless of activity (default: 8 hours)
- **Actions requiring re-verification** — Sensitive actions that require you to confirm your identity before proceeding (for example, approving clinical records or exporting PDFs)

**Access recertification** — A periodic review process where administrators verify that each team member still needs their current level of access.

**Consent status** — Tracks whether patient consent has been obtained for a session. Available choices:
- **Pending** — Consent has not yet been addressed
- **Confirmed** — Consent has been confirmed
- **Obtained** — Consent has been formally obtained
- **Revoked** — The patient has withdrawn consent
