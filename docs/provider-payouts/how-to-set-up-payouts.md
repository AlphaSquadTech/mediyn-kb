# How to Set Up Payouts

Mediyn guides you through a secure onboarding process to connect your payout account and start receiving earnings.

## Steps

1. Go to **Settings** in Mediyn.
2. Select **Payouts** or **Payout Account**.
3. Select **Set Up Payouts**.
4. Mediyn will open a secure onboarding page.
5. Follow the prompts to provide your business and banking information.
6. Once you complete all steps, you will be redirected back to Mediyn.

## You'll Need to Provide

During onboarding, you will be asked for:
- Your business or personal details
- Your bank account information
- Verification documents (if required)

## What to Expect

After completing the onboarding process:
- Your payout account status changes to **Active**.
- Payouts and charges are enabled for your account.
- Any earnings that Mediyn was holding for you will be released.
- Future patient payments will flow to your payout account automatically, minus the platform fee.

## If Your Onboarding Link Expires

The onboarding link is valid for a limited time. If it expires before you finish:

1. Go back to **Settings** and select **Payouts**.
2. Select **Resume Setup**.
3. Mediyn will generate a new onboarding link so you can continue.

## Good to Know

- Only clinic administrators can set up the payout account.
- If you do not complete onboarding right away, you can resume later. Your progress is saved.
- Mediyn safely holds your earnings until your payout account is active. No earnings are lost.
- You only need to complete onboarding once. After that, payouts happen automatically.
