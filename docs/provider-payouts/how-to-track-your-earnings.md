# How to Track Your Earnings

Mediyn provides a clear view of your payout balance, recent payouts, platform fees, and any held amounts.

![Billing Settings](../images/clinic/settings-billing.png)

## Checking Your Payout Status

1. Go to **Settings** in Mediyn.
2. Select **Payouts**.
3. You will see an overview of your payout account, including:
   - **Account status** — Whether your account is active, restricted, or in another stage
   - **Payouts enabled** — Whether payouts are currently flowing to your account
   - **Charges enabled** — Whether patient payments can be processed

## Viewing Your Balance

On the Payouts page, you will see your current balance breakdown. This shows how much is available for payout and any pending amounts.

## Viewing Recent Payouts

The Payouts page also shows your recent payout history. Each entry includes the amount and date of the payout.

## Understanding Platform Fees

Mediyn deducts a small platform fee from each patient payment. You can see your current platform fee percentage on the Payouts page. The fee is applied automatically. The remaining amount goes to your payout account.

## Viewing Held Amounts

If you have not yet connected a payout account, Mediyn holds your earnings safely. The held amount is displayed on the Payouts page. Once you complete onboarding, these funds will be released.

## Accessing Your Detailed Payout Dashboard

For more detailed financial information:

1. Go to the **Payouts** page.
2. Select **View Payout Dashboard**.
3. Mediyn opens a secure dashboard where you can review detailed payout history, tax forms, and more.

## Good to Know

- Only clinic administrators can access payout details.
- The payout dashboard link is single-use for security. Request a new link each time you want to access it.
- If your account shows a **Restricted** status, check your payout dashboard for details on what needs to be resolved.
- Payouts are processed automatically. You do not need to manually request a payout.
