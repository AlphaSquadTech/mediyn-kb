# Provider Payouts

Mediyn handles payment processing and payouts so therapists and clinics receive their earnings from patient payments.

## What You Can Do

- Connect your payout account through a guided onboarding process
- Check your payout account status at any time
- View your current balance and recent payouts
- Access your payout dashboard for detailed financial information
- Track platform fees and held amounts

## Key Concepts

**Payout account** — A connected financial account where Mediyn sends your earnings. You set this up through a secure onboarding process.

**Payout account stages** — Your account moves through these stages:
- **Not connected** — You have not started the onboarding process yet
- **Onboarding** — You have started setup but have not finished providing all required information
- **Active** — Your account is fully set up and ready to receive payouts
- **Restricted** — There is an issue with your account that needs attention before payouts can continue
- **Disabled** — Your payout account has been deactivated

**Platform fee** — A small percentage that Mediyn deducts from each patient payment for using the platform. The remainder goes to your payout account.

**Held amount** — If you have not yet connected a payout account, Mediyn holds your earnings safely. Once you complete onboarding, held funds will be released to your account.

**Payouts enabled** — Indicates whether your account can currently receive payouts.

**Charges enabled** — Indicates whether payments can be processed through your account.
