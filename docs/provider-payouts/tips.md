# Tips for Provider Payouts

## Recommendations

- **Complete onboarding as soon as possible** — The sooner you connect your payout account, the sooner you start receiving earnings directly. Until then, Mediyn holds your funds safely.

- **Keep your banking details up to date** — If your bank account changes, update your information through the payout dashboard to avoid payout delays.

- **Check your payout status regularly** — A quick check confirms that payouts and charges are both enabled. If either is turned off, review your account for any required actions.

- **Review your platform fee** — Understand the percentage Mediyn deducts from each payment so you can set your session rates accordingly.

- **Use the payout dashboard for tax records** — Your payout dashboard includes detailed history and tax forms that are useful at tax time.

- **Address restrictions promptly** — If your account is marked as Restricted, log in to your payout dashboard right away. Resolving issues quickly prevents payout interruptions.

## Common Questions

**Q: When will I receive my payouts?**
A: Payouts are processed automatically on a regular schedule once your account is active. Check your payout dashboard for specific timing.

**Q: What happens to my earnings if I have not connected a payout account?**
A: Mediyn holds your earnings safely. Once you complete the onboarding process, held funds will be released to your account.

**Q: Can I change my bank account later?**
A: Yes. Access your payout dashboard from the Payouts page in Mediyn and update your banking details there.

**Q: Who can manage payout settings?**
A: Only clinic administrators can set up and manage payout accounts.

**Q: How is the platform fee calculated?**
A: Mediyn deducts a percentage from each patient payment. The exact rate is shown on your Payouts page. The remaining amount is sent to your payout account.
