# How to Manage Conversations

Mediyn helps you stay organized by providing tools to view conversation history and keep track of your messaging threads.

![Messaging](../images/clinic/messaging.png)

## Viewing Conversation Details

1. Open the **Messages** section.
2. Select a conversation to view its full details.

### What to Expect

- You see the therapist name, patient name, and when the conversation was created.
- The last message preview and unread count are shown.
- The full message history is available inside the conversation.

## Browsing Message History

1. Open a conversation.
2. Scroll through messages to review the full history.

### What to Expect

- Messages appear in chronological order.
- Each message shows the sender, content, attachments, and date and time.
- If the conversation has many messages, use the next page of results to load older messages.

## Staying on Top of Unread Messages

- Your conversation list shows the **unread count** for each thread.
- Conversations with unread messages stand out so you can prioritize them.
- Opening a conversation marks its messages as read.

## For Patients

Patients manage their conversations in the same way from their own account:

- They see a list of all their conversations.
- Each thread shows the unread count and last message preview.
- They can browse full message history within each conversation.

## Good to Know

- Conversation history is preserved indefinitely. You can always go back and review past communications.
- The unread count resets as soon as you open the conversation and view the messages.
- Clinic administrators can view any conversation in the practice for compliance and oversight purposes.
