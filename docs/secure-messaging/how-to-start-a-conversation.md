# How to Start a Conversation

Mediyn lets you create secure messaging threads with your patients for between-session communication.

![Messaging](../images/clinic/messaging.png)

## Creating a New Conversation

1. Go to the **Messages** section in Mediyn.
2. Choose **New Conversation**.

You'll need to provide:
- **Therapist** -- The therapist for this conversation (typically yourself)
- **Patient** -- The patient you want to message

### What to Expect

- A new conversation thread is created between the therapist and the patient.
- Both participants can see the conversation in their message list.
- The conversation shows the therapist name, patient name, and the date it was created.

## Finding Existing Conversations

1. Open the **Messages** section.
2. Browse your conversation list.

You can also:
- **Search by patient** -- Narrow down your conversations to find a specific patient's thread

### What to Expect

- Your conversations are listed with the most recently active ones first.
- Each conversation shows a preview of the last message and the number of unread messages.
- If you have many conversations, use the next page of results to see more.

## Good to Know

- Each therapist-patient pair has its own conversation thread. All messages between you and a specific patient appear in one place.
- Patients can also see their conversations from their own account. They do not need to start a new conversation -- they can simply reply to an existing one.
- Clinic administrators can view all conversations across the practice.
