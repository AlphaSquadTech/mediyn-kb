# How to Create a Clinic Account

Register your clinic or solo practice on Mediyn to start managing appointments, patients, and your team.

## Creating a Clinic Account

Use this option if you are setting up a practice with multiple therapists.

### Steps

1. Go to the Mediyn signup page.
2. Choose **Clinic** as your account type.
3. You'll need to provide:
   - **Email address** — Your admin email for the clinic.
   - **Password** — Must include an uppercase letter, a lowercase letter, a number, and a special character.
   - **Clinic name** — The name of your practice.
   - **Time zone** — Your clinic's local time zone.
4. You can also:
   - **Choose a subscription plan** — Select the plan that fits your practice. If you skip this, Mediyn starts you on the default clinic trial.
5. Select **Create Account**.
6. If your chosen plan requires payment details, Mediyn will guide you to a secure checkout page to enter your card.

## Creating a Solo Therapist Account

Use this option if you are an independent therapist working on your own.

### Steps

1. Go to the Mediyn signup page.
2. Choose **Solo Therapist** as your account type.
3. You'll need to provide:
   - **Email address** — Your professional email.
   - **Password** — Must include an uppercase letter, a lowercase letter, a number, and a special character.
   - **First name** and **Last name**.
   - **Time zone** — Your local time zone.
4. You can also:
   - **Choose a subscription plan** — Options like Starter or Professional are available. If you skip this, Mediyn starts you on the default solo therapist trial.
5. Select **Create Account**.
6. If your chosen plan requires payment details, Mediyn will guide you to a secure checkout page.

## Accepting an Invitation

If you received an invitation to join an existing clinic, follow these steps instead.

### Steps

1. Open the invitation link from your email.
2. You'll need to provide:
   - **First name** and **Last name**.
   - **Password** — Must include an uppercase letter, a lowercase letter, a number, and a special character.
3. Select **Accept Invitation**.

## What to Expect

- After creating your account, you are automatically signed in.
- If two-step verification is required, you will complete that step before reaching your dashboard.
- Mediyn may guide you through an onboarding setup to configure your practice. See [Getting Started & Setup](../getting-started-setup/overview.md) for details.

## Good to Know

- Each email address can only be used for one Mediyn account.
- You can always change your subscription plan later.
- Invited team members join under your existing clinic. They do not need to create a separate clinic account.
