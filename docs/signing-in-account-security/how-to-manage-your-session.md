# How to Manage Your Session

Keep your Mediyn session active while you work and sign out securely when you are done.

![Security Settings](../images/clinic/settings-security.png)

## Refreshing Your Session

Your session stays active while you use Mediyn. The platform refreshes it in the background automatically.

- You do not need to do anything. Mediyn keeps you signed in during active use.
- If your session expires, you will be asked to sign in again.

## Signing Out

1. Open the account menu in Mediyn.
2. Select **Sign Out**.
3. Your session ends immediately.

## What to Expect

- After signing out, you will need to sign in again with your email and password.
- If two-step verification is enabled, you will complete that step again on your next sign-in.

## Good to Know

- Always sign out when using a shared or public computer.
- If you close the browser without signing out, your session will expire on its own after a period of inactivity.
- Signing out on one device does not affect sessions on other devices.
