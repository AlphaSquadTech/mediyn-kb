# How to Sign In

Use your email and password to access your Mediyn account.

![Security Settings](../images/clinic/settings-security.png)

## Steps

1. Open the Mediyn sign-in page.
2. Enter your **email address**.
3. Enter your **password**.
4. Select **Sign In**.

## What to Expect

- If two-step verification is turned on, you will be asked to complete a verification step. See [How to Set Up Two-Step Verification](how-to-set-up-two-step-verification.md) for details.
- If your subscription requires payment setup, Mediyn will guide you to a checkout page before you continue.
- Once verified, you are signed in and taken to your dashboard.

## Good to Know

- If you enter the wrong password, Mediyn will show an error. Double-check your credentials and try again.
- Your sign-in session will expire after a period of time. Mediyn can refresh it automatically so you stay signed in during active use.
- If you signed up through the Mediyn website and are moving to the Mediyn app, your session transfers automatically. No need to sign in again.
