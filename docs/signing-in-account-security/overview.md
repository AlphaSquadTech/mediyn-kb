# Signing In & Account Security

Mediyn provides secure sign-in and account protection features to keep your clinic data safe.

![Security Settings](../images/clinic/settings-security.png)

## What You Can Do

- Sign in with your email and password
- Add two-step verification for extra security
- Refresh your session to stay signed in
- Sign out when you are done
- Create a new clinic or solo therapist account
- Accept an invitation to join an existing clinic

## Key Concepts

- **Two-step verification** — An extra security step after your password. You confirm your identity with a code from an authenticator app or a text message.
- **Session** — The period during which you stay signed in. Sessions expire after a set time for security.
- **Subscription plan** — The pricing tier you choose when creating your account. A free trial is available by default.
