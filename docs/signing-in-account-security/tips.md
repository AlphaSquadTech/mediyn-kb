# Tips for Signing In & Account Security

## Recommendations

- **Enable two-step verification** — This is the single most effective way to protect your account. Even if someone learns your password, they cannot sign in without the verification code.
- **Use a strong, unique password** — Include uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Do not reuse passwords from other services.
- **Sign out on shared devices** — Always sign out when you finish working on a computer that others can access.
- **Keep your authenticator app backed up** — If you use an authenticator app for two-step verification, make sure you have a backup or recovery method in case you lose your phone.
- **Check your email address is current** — Your email is used for sign-in and account recovery. Keep it up to date.

## Common Questions

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Use the password reset option on the sign-in page. Mediyn will send a reset link to your email.

**Q: Can I change my email address after signing up?**
A: Contact your clinic administrator or Mediyn support to update your email.

**Q: What happens if my verification code expires?**
A: You can request a new code. The previous code will no longer work.

**Q: Do I need two-step verification to use Mediyn?**
A: It depends on your clinic's security settings. Even if it is not required, we strongly recommend enabling it.

**Q: Can I be signed in on multiple devices at the same time?**
A: Yes. Each device maintains its own session. Signing out on one device does not affect the others.
