# How to Generate Superbills

Mediyn creates superbills for completed sessions and automatically fills in provider and patient information.

## Steps

1. Open the session you want to create a superbill for.
2. Select **Create Superbill**.
3. Review and confirm the details.
4. Save the superbill.

## You'll Need to Provide

- **Diagnosis codes** — One or more ICD-10 codes for the patient's diagnosis

## You Can Also

- **Override the procedure code** — Mediyn pre-fills the CPT procedure code based on the session type. You can change it if needed.

## What to Expect

Mediyn automatically populates the superbill with:
- Your name, NPI number, and tax identification number
- The patient's name, date of birth, and address
- The patient's insurance information (if on file)
- The session date and amount charged
- The currency

The superbill is created in **Draft** stage. You can review it before finalizing.

## Viewing Your Superbills

1. Go to the **Superbills** section in Mediyn.
2. You will see a list of all superbills.
3. Narrow down your results using these options:
   - **Patient** — Show superbills for a specific patient
   - **Status** — Show only Draft, Finalized, or Voided superbills
4. Select any superbill to view its full details.

## Voiding a Superbill

If a superbill was created in error:

1. Open the superbill.
2. Select **Void Superbill**.
3. The superbill moves to the **Voided** stage and can no longer be used.

## Good to Know

- Only therapists and clinic administrators can create superbills.
- Only clinic administrators can void superbills.
- Mediyn can automatically create superbills when sessions are approved. Enable this in your billing settings.
- Each superbill is tied to one session. You cannot combine multiple sessions into a single superbill.
- Make sure your NPI and tax identification numbers are up to date in your profile. These appear on every superbill.
