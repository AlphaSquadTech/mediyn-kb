# How to Manage Therapist Profiles

View, find, and update therapist details in Mediyn.

![Therapist Profile](../images/clinic/settings-profile.png)

## Viewing All Therapists

### Steps

1. Sign in to your Mediyn clinic admin account.
2. Navigate to the **Therapists** section.
3. Browse the list of therapists in your clinic.

### What to Expect

- You will see a list of all therapists in your clinic.
- Results are shown in pages. Use the navigation controls to load more if you have a large team.
- The list shows each therapist's basic details, including name and status.

## Viewing a Therapist's Full Profile

### Steps

1. From the therapists list, select a therapist's name.
2. View their complete profile.

### What to Expect

You will see the following details:

- **Name** — First name, last name, and preferred name (if set).
- **Display name** — The name shown throughout Mediyn.
- **Email address**.
- **Role** — Clinic Admin or Therapist.
- **Status** — Active, Suspended, or Deactivated.
- **Session duration preference** — Their preferred appointment length in minutes.
- **Professional credentials** — Qualifications such as LCSW, PsyD, or similar.
- **NPI** — National Provider Identifier number.
- **Tax ID**.
- **License number** and **License state**.

## Updating a Therapist's Profile

### Steps

1. Open the therapist's profile.
2. Select **Edit** or a similar option.
3. You can update any of the following:
   - **First name**, **Last name**, and **Preferred name**.
   - **Session duration preference** — Change their default appointment length.
   - **Professional credentials** — Update qualifications.
   - **NPI** — National Provider Identifier.
   - **Tax ID**.
   - **License number** and **License state**.
   - **Status** — Change to one of the following:
     - **Active** — Restore full access.
     - **Suspended** — Temporarily pause access.
     - **Deactivated** — Remove access entirely.
4. Save your changes.

### What to Expect

- The updated profile is saved immediately.
- The display name updates automatically based on the preferred name or first and last name.

## Good to Know

- Only clinic administrators can view and update other therapists' profiles.
- Therapists can view and edit their own profile details.
- The display name cannot be set directly. It is calculated from the preferred name (if provided) or the first and last name.
- Changing a therapist's status to Suspended or Deactivated affects their ability to sign in and access Mediyn.
