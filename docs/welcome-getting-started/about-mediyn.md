# About Mediyn

Mediyn is a practice management platform designed specifically for therapy and mental health clinics.

![Mediyn Dashboard](../images/clinic/dashboard.png)

## Built for Mental Health Practices

Mediyn helps therapists and clinic teams manage every part of their day. From the moment a patient books a session to the final note and invoice, Mediyn keeps everything connected.

You can use Mediyn whether you are a solo practitioner or part of a larger group practice. Each clinic gets its own private space within Mediyn. Your data stays separate and secure from other practices on the platform.

## The Full Session Lifecycle

Mediyn covers every step of a therapy session, from start to finish.

- **Before the session** -- Patients complete intake forms, you review their information, and the session is scheduled on your calendar.
- **During the session** -- You can record the session securely. Mediyn keeps recordings linked to the patient record.
- **After the session** -- Write your clinical notes, assign worksheets, and send follow-up messages. Notes go through a review and approval process to keep your documentation thorough.
- **Billing and payment** -- Generate invoices, accept payments, and create superbills for insurance reimbursement. Mediyn handles payment processing through a secure payment partner.

## Your Clinic, Your Space

Every clinic on Mediyn operates in its own private space. This means:

- Your patient records are visible only to your team
- Your settings, templates, and preferences are unique to your practice
- You control who has access and what they can see
- Your billing and financial data remain private to your clinic

## Mediyn on the Go

Mediyn includes a companion app for iPhone and iPad. The mobile app lets therapists and clinic staff stay connected while away from their desk.

You can check your schedule, review patient details, and manage key tasks from your mobile device.

## Secure Payment Processing

Mediyn partners with a trusted payment provider to handle billing securely. You can:

- Accept credit and debit card payments from patients
- Set up subscription plans for ongoing care
- Sell session packages at bundled rates
- Track payment history and outstanding balances
- Generate professional invoices automatically

Your financial information is handled with the same care and security as your clinical data.
