# Quick Start Guide

Follow these steps to set up your Mediyn account and start seeing patients.

## Your First-Time Setup Checklist

Getting started with Mediyn takes just a few steps. Work through this list in order to make sure everything is ready.

### Step 1: Sign In and Secure Your Account

Sign in to Mediyn using the credentials provided by your clinic administrator. Once you are logged in, set up two-step verification right away. This adds an extra layer of protection to your account.

You will need to provide:

- Your email address
- Your password
- A verification code from your phone or authenticator app

### Step 2: Complete Your Onboarding

Mediyn will walk you through a short onboarding process. If you are a clinic administrator, you will set up your clinic details first. If you are a therapist, you will complete your professional profile.

During onboarding, you can:

- Add your clinic name, address, and contact information
- Upload your professional photo
- Enter your credentials and specialties
- Set your preferred time zone

### Step 3: Add Your First Patient

Create a patient record to start building your caseload. You will need basic details like the patient's name, contact information, and date of birth.

You can also send an intake form to your patient. This lets them fill in their information before the first visit.

### Step 4: Set Up Your Availability

Tell Mediyn when you are available to see patients. Set your working hours for each day of the week. You can also block off time for breaks, meetings, or personal tasks.

Your availability controls what time slots patients and staff can book.

### Step 5: Schedule Your First Session

Book a session with your patient. Pick a date and time that fits your availability. Choose the session type and add any notes for yourself.

Your patient will receive a notification about the upcoming session.

### Step 6: Configure Billing

Set up your billing preferences so you can accept payments. Connect your payment account, create your service rates, and choose how you want to invoice patients.

You can also:

- Create session packages for bundled pricing
- Set up subscription plans for ongoing care
- Enable automatic invoice generation

## Key Actions to Try First

Once you have completed the setup checklist, explore these features.

- **Record a session** -- Try the recording feature during a session to see how it works.
- **Write a clinical note** -- After a session, create your first clinical note using one of the available templates.
- **Send a message** -- Use secure messaging to send a follow-up to your patient.
- **Assign a worksheet** -- Browse the worksheet library and assign one to a patient.
- **Review your dashboard** -- Check your dashboard to see your upcoming schedule and recent activity.

## Where to Get Help

If you need assistance at any time, here is where to look.

- **This knowledge base** -- Browse by topic or search for specific questions.
- **FAQ section** -- Check the Frequently Asked Questions for quick answers to common issues.
- **Your clinic administrator** -- They can help with account access, permissions, and clinic-level settings.
- **Mediyn support** -- Reach out to the Mediyn support team for additional help.

You are all set. Welcome to Mediyn.
