# How to Approve and Download a Worksheet

Once your worksheet draft is ready, approve it to create the final version and download the PDF.

## Steps

1. Open the worksheet draft you want to finalize.
2. Choose **Approve Worksheet**.

You can also:
- **Assign to session** -- Automatically link the approved worksheet to the current session

### What to Expect

- Mediyn creates a final, approved artifact linked to your session.
- A download link for the PDF is provided immediately.
- The download link is temporary. It expires after a short time.

## Downloading the PDF

- When you approve a worksheet, the download link appears right away. Use it to save the PDF.
- If the link has expired, you can get a new download link at any time from the approved worksheet.

### What to Expect

- The download provides the finalized, de-identified PDF.
- Each download link is temporary and will expire. Simply get a new one if needed.

## Viewing Approved Worksheets

You can view all approved worksheets in several ways:

- **By session** -- See all approved worksheets for a specific session
- **Delivered tab** -- Browse all approved worksheets across your patients
- You can narrow down by patient, therapist, or assignment status (assigned or unassigned)

### What to Expect

- Therapists see approved worksheets for their assigned patients only.
- Clinic administrators can see all approved worksheets across the practice.

## Good to Know

- You can approve multiple worksheets for the same session. Each one addresses different clinical concerns.
- Approved worksheets are permanent records. If you need to make changes, use the replace feature to create a new version.
- The download link expires for security reasons. You can always get a fresh link from the approved worksheet.
