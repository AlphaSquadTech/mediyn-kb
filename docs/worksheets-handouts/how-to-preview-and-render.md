# How to Preview and Render a Worksheet

Before finalizing a worksheet, you can preview it to check for warnings and review the de-identification report, then render a print-ready PDF.

![Worksheet Templates](../images/clinic/worksheets-templates.png)

## Previewing a Draft

1. Open the worksheet draft you want to review.
2. Choose **Preview Worksheet**.
3. Mediyn builds a fresh preview and checks the content for issues.

### What to Expect

- You will see the current state of the draft (ready for preview, warning present, etc.).
- Any **warnings** are listed so you can review flagged content.
- A **de-identification report** shows what identifying information was detected and how it was handled.

## Rendering a PDF

1. From the draft, choose **Render PDF**.
2. Mediyn generates a print-ready, de-identified worksheet in PDF format.

### What to Expect

- The PDF is created with all patient privacy protections applied.
- You will also see any warnings and the de-identification report.
- If rendering succeeds without issues, the draft moves forward and is ready for approval.

## Handling Warnings

Some warnings require your acknowledgement before Mediyn can finish rendering.

1. If the PDF rendering flags warnings that need acknowledgement, Mediyn will let you know which warnings must be addressed.
2. Review each warning carefully.
3. Acknowledge the warnings and re-render the PDF.

You can acknowledge warnings in two ways:
- **Edit the draft** -- Update the flagged fields and acknowledge warnings directly on the draft
- **Acknowledge during rendering** -- Provide your acknowledgements when you render the PDF again

### What to Expect

- Once all required warnings are acknowledged, the PDF renders successfully.
- Warnings that do not require acknowledgement are informational only. They will not block rendering.

## Good to Know

- You do not have to preview separately before rendering. The render step includes the same warning and de-identification checks.
- If you want to inspect warnings without creating a PDF, use the standalone preview option.
- De-identification protects your patient's privacy by flagging or removing personal details from the worksheet content.
