# Worksheets & Handouts

Mediyn helps you create, customize, and share therapeutic worksheets with your patients.

## What You Can Do

- Browse a catalog of worksheet templates by therapy domain, modality, or complexity
- Get personalized template recommendations based on session notes and clinical assessment data
- Create worksheet drafts tailored to each patient's needs
- Preview drafts and review de-identification reports before finalizing
- Render print-ready PDFs with patient privacy protections built in
- Approve worksheets and download them instantly
- Assign worksheets to patients and track their progress
- Review patient responses once they complete a worksheet
- Replace an approved worksheet with a newer version when needed

## Key Concepts

- **Template**: A pre-built worksheet structure you choose from the catalog. Templates come in different therapy domains and complexity levels.
- **Draft**: A personalized version of a template, created for a specific session. You can edit drafts before approving them.
- **De-identification**: Mediyn automatically checks your worksheet for any identifying information that should be removed before sharing with a patient.
- **Warnings**: During preview or PDF rendering, Mediyn may flag content that needs your attention. Some warnings require your acknowledgement before you can proceed.
- **Artifact**: The final, approved version of a worksheet linked to a session. This is what gets shared with your patient.
- **Assignment**: When you send an approved worksheet to a patient. You can track whether they have started, submitted, or need review.

## Worksheet Stages

Your worksheet moves through these stages:

1. **Draft Created** -- The initial draft is generated from a template
2. **Ready for Preview** -- The draft is ready for you to review
3. **Warning Present** -- The draft has flagged content that needs your attention
4. **Approved** -- You have approved the worksheet and it is ready to share
5. **Assigned** -- The worksheet has been sent to a patient

## Assignment Stages

Once you assign a worksheet to a patient, it moves through these stages:

1. **Assigned** -- The patient has received the worksheet
2. **In Progress** -- The patient has started working on it
3. **Submitted** -- The patient has finished and sent it back
4. **Reviewed** -- You have reviewed the patient's responses
